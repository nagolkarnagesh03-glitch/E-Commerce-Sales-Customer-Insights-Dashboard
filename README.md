# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis solution for an e-commerce dataset using Excel, SQL, and Power BI. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze sales trends over time
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on revenue and profit
* Provide insights for better business decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning)
* SQL (Data Analysis)
* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## 📁 Dataset Features

* Order Details (Order ID, Date, Status)
* Customer Information (Name, Location)
* Product Details (Category, Brand, Quantity)
* Pricing Metrics (Unit Price, Discount, Tax, Total Amount)
* Payment Methods

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Page 1: Sales Overview

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Orders, Customers, AOV)
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top Products Analysis
* Category Distribution
* Sales by Country
* Interactive Filters (Category, Date, Payment)

---

### 🔹 Page 2: Customer & Profit Analysis

* Top Customers by Revenue
* Payment Method Distribution
* Discount Impact Analysis
* Order Status Breakdown
* Profit by Category

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Top-performing products contribute significantly to total revenue
* Majority of orders are successfully delivered (~75%)
* Higher discounts increase sales volume but reduce profitability
* Credit Card and UPI are widely used payment methods
* Electronics category generates the highest profit
